Your Questions Answered

Everything You're Wondering

Because your first dance isn't a performance — it's a moment between you and the person you love. Tomas is a World Champion who's been on Dancing With The Stars and in Disney's Enchanted — but honestly? His favorite stage is a small studio with two people who just want their dance to feel right. He doesn't just teach. He feels it with you.

Go ahead — tell him you can't dance. He loves hearing that. It's his favorite challenge. She's nervous, he hasn't danced since middle school, neither of them knows where to start — Tomas has heard it a thousand times. And a thousand times they've walked out of that first lesson looking at each other like "wait… are we actually dancing right now?" Yes. Yes you are.

Want something that makes the whole room go silent? Tell him. Want lifts, dips, something cinematic that nobody saw coming? Say the word. The more you want, the more he brings. Tomas has choreographed for world stages and Hollywood — if you want simple and elegant, it'll be beautiful. But if you want to go big? You just woke up the champion.

Forgot everything the night before? Call him. Nerves hit at midnight? Text him. Freaking out five minutes before your song plays? He's right there. Tomas doesn't teach you and disappear — he's with you every step of the way. Because honestly? He's almost as excited about your moment as you are.
